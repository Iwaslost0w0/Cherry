Who are we?

My name is Yaroslav Minenko. My goal is to increase the level of information security in Russia. Due to the computerization of our country, cases of hacking and use of other people's personal data are increasing. My goal is to reduce the cases of hacking and theft of personal data by creating a special website capable of finding verified information. Any information on the site is carefully checked before installing on the site.
